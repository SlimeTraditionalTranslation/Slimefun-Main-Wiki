The Carbon Press is an [Electric Machine](../Electric-Machines.md) which can be used to craft all [Carbon](../../Resources/Elements-and-compounds/Carbon.md)-like items.

## Carbon Press Tiers

| Tier | Power   | Speed Multiplier |
| :--: | :-----: | :--------------: |
| I    | 20 J/s  | 1.0x             |
| II   | 50 J/s  | 3.0x             |
| III  | 180 J/s | 15.0x            |
