The Carbon Press is an [Electric Machine](Electric-Machines) which can be used to craft all [Carbon](Carbon)-like items.

## Carbon Press Tiers

| Tier |  Power  | Speed Multiplier |
|:----:|:-------:|:----------------:|
|  I   | 20 J/s  |       1.0x       |
|  II  | 50 J/s  |       3.0x       |
| III  | 180 J/s |      15.0x       |
