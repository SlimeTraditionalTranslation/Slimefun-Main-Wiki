Meat Jerky is a [Food](/docs/slimefun/food) item added by Slimefun. Using an [Enhanced Crafting Table](/docs/slimefun/enhanced-crafting-table), Beef Jerky, Pork Jerky, Chicken Jerky, Mutton Jerky and Fish Jerky can be made from salt and the corresponding meat.

When consumed, it fully replenishes saturation.
